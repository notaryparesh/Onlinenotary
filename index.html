FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about notary services and procedures.

What documents can be notarized?

Various legal documents can be notarized including affidavits, power of attorney, property documents, wills, agreements, contracts, educational certificates, and more. If you're unsure about a specific document, please contact us for guidance.

What identification is required for notarization?

You need to provide a valid government-issued photo ID such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Passport, or Voter ID. The identification should clearly show your photograph, signature, and full name.

How much does notarization cost?

Notary fees in India are regulated and vary based on the type and value of the document. General notarization typically ranges from Rs. 10 to Rs. 50 per document. For specific fee information, please contact our office.

How long does the notarization process take?

Most standard notarizations can be completed within 30 minutes to an hour if all required documents are in order. Complex cases may take longer. We offer same-day service for most notarizations.

Can you notarize documents in languages other than English or Hindi?

Yes, we can notarize documents in various languages. However, for languages other than English, Hindi, or Gujarati, a certified translation may be required along with the original document.
